Inside Kangri Dham

Image shows the prepration process

The menu starts with-
  1. Rice, Moong dal, Rajma (Red Kidney Beans), Chole (Chickpea) is cooked in Desi Ghee which is known as ‘Madrah’.
  2. It is then followed by preparing ‘Maash dal’ which is mixed with three types of dals- Moong dal, Urad dal, Masoor dal.
  3. It is then topped with Khatta, which is prepared by Jaggery and Tamarind in an iron vessel.  
  4. Finally, ‘Mitha Bhaat’ is served which is a sweet made of either sweet rice or bread crumbs in yoghurt.
